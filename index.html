Two prep sessions, three things to wash

1
Sunday — ~50 minutesRice cooker: full week's rice (630g dry = 7 × 90g portions). Spread to cool within the hour, box up, freeze all 7. While it cooks: brown 300g mince with paprika + cumin in the wok, scramble in 6 eggs, toss through 3 thawed rice portions + peas — that's Mon–Wed lunches boxed. Air-fry 3 breasts + sweet potato/wedges for Mon–Wed dinners if you want dinners boxed too, or leave dinners to the air fryer fresh (10 min, foil liner, nothing to wash). Load 3 Creami tubs into the freezer.
2
Wednesday — ~30 minutesSame again for Thu–Sun: 400g mince, 8 eggs, 4 rice portions, 4 Creami tubs. Cooked food sits in the fridge a maximum of 3 days, which is why the week splits 3 + 4.
3
Every day — zero pansBreakfast is a bowl and a spoon. Lunch is a microwave box (piping hot, reheat once only). Dinner is the air fryer with a liner. Creami spins in its own tub. The only real washing up all week: the wok twice, the rice cooker pot once, the air fryer basket.
Rice safety: cool it fast (within 1 hour), freeze or fridge straight away, reheat until piping hot, never reheat twice. Frozen portions microwave from frozen in ~3 minutes and are the safest way to batch rice.
